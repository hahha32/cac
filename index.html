The new school 2023 graduates' statistics

the graduating class of 2023 at The New School represents a diverse and dynamic group of students and this data visualization explores key statistics about these graduates. By analyzing this data, we gain a clearer understanding of who these graduates are and the paths they’ve taken.

Have fun.

The Majors

Each of the 12 dots on this graph represents one of the top 12 majors among The New School's 2023 graduates.

Departments

The four colors of the dots correspond to The New School's academic divisions—including Parsons School of Design, The New School for Social Research, School of Media Studies, and the College of Performing Arts The New School for Social Research, School of Media Studies, or Parsons School of Design.

Amount

The size of each dot corresponds to the number of 2023 graduates from that major—larger dots indicate higher graduation numbers, allowing you to quickly compare the most and least populated fields of study.

Citations

The position of each dot along the horizontal position (x-axis) reflects the average number of citations per published paper by students in that major, highlighting which fields generate the most influential research.

Position y by magnitude

The vertical position (y-axis) of each dot represents the post-graduation freelance or self-employment rate for that major—higher dots indicate fields where graduates are more likely to pursue independent work.
